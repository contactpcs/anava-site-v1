Frequently asked questions

If you don't find your answer here, simply use the form above.

No referral is required to make an enquiry or book an initial consultation. However, we strongly encourage coordination with your existing healthcare team. If you have a psychiatrist, GP, or neurologist, we are happy to liaise with them directly.
Your first consultation is an unhurried conversation. A clinician will take a thorough history, ask about your symptoms, treatment history, and goals. They will explain which neurotherapy options may be relevant to you, what the evidence suggests, and what a treatment course would involve. There is no pressure to proceed — the goal is information and understanding.
Yes, there is a consultation fee. Our team will provide full details when you contact us. Treatment pricing is provided following your assessment, once a personalised protocol has been designed.
This varies significantly by condition and individual response. A standard TMS course for depression typically involves 20–30 sessions over 4–6 weeks, attended daily. Other protocols may be shorter or structured differently. Your clinician will discuss expected session numbers after your assessment, and the protocol will be adapted as your treatment progresses.
In most cases, yes. Neurotherapy does not interact with most medications and can often be used alongside existing pharmacotherapy or psychological therapy. Your clinician will review your current medications at consultation and advise on any considerations. Never stop or change medication without medical supervision.
