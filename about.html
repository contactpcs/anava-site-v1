Our story

Built for the patients
who were told there was nothing more.

ANAVA exists because too many patients reach the end of conventional treatment pathways without having exhausted the possibilities of non-invasive neurotherapy.

"The technology exists. Access shouldn't be the barrier."

Why ANAVA exists

A clinic designed from
the patient's perspective

The idea for ANAVA grew from a simple, frustrating observation: evidence-based brain stimulation therapies had a growing body of peer-reviewed research behind them — but they remained largely inaccessible to the patients who needed them most.

Patients with treatment-resistant depression, chronic pain, and neurological conditions were sometimes waiting years to access TMS. When they did find it, it was often in settings that felt clinical, rushed, or impersonal — environments that treated the procedure rather than the person.

ANAVA was designed to be different. Not because difference is a brand value, but because the evidence suggests that the environment in which treatment is delivered — the quality of the therapeutic relationship, the clarity of communication, the safety a patient feels — matters to outcomes.

We built the clinic we wished had existed for our patients.

Our philosophy

Clinically rigorous.
Emotionally intelligent.
Beautifully human.

Evidence first

We only offer therapies that have meaningful peer-reviewed evidence behind them. We stay current with the research and we never overstate what the evidence supports.

Honesty above hope

We will not promise what we cannot deliver. Patients who have already been through difficult treatment journeys deserve honest communication — not inflated expectations.

Patient as person

Every clinical decision is made in the context of a whole person — their history, their life, their goals. We do not treat diagnoses. We treat people.
